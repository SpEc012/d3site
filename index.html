What we do

D3 Inspection specializes in field services, offering a comprehensive range of solutions tailored to our clients' needs — pipeline and construction inspection, environmental inspection, project and construction management, and Veriforce Operator Qualification training for pipeline construction personnel.

We take pride in a team of professionals committed to excellence and the highest industry standards. Our personnel are carefully selected and undergo rigorous training and certification to ensure they're qualified to complete any assigned task.

Our focus is ensuring every construction activity meets regulatory requirements and industry standards. Our inspectors have a thorough understanding of PHMSA, DOT, OSHA, and state and local regulations, as well as client specifications — so every aspect of the work complies with the highest safety and quality standards.
